God's Eye

God’s eye is an artificial intelligence based web application/platform for blind children to help them perform their everyday chores using a voice assistant without help from anyone. This platform helps them to interact with the environment, enables them to learn from their surrounding by identifying various things and scenes around them, and also aids them in recognizing their friends and family. To utilize this application, the visually impaired person needs to speak or tap on the device to perform a particular task. So, it is pretty easy to use once configured as per the requirement.

About God's Eye

God’s eye is an AI-based web application for blind children to help them perform their everyday chores themselves using a voice assistant and without any other help from anyone. This platform helps them to interact with the environment, enables them to learn from their surrounding by identifying various things around them, and also aids them in recognizing their friends and family.

The web application mainly helps children learn through web surfing, reading books through OCR, enrolling in miscellaneous online courses, and listening to audiobooks. College graduates can even search for suitable jobs and apply for them through this portal. Moreover, they can have an online session with a counsellor if they find some difficulty and want to discuss it.

To lighten the mood, children can listen to music or jokes. In addition, they can send a message to a friend (if they want) through this medium. The other significant features of this web application include listening to the news, a calculator, and information regarding date, time, and weather.

Our Vision

Visually impaired children often find it challenging to carry out their typical daily activities. So, they require permanent and continuous assistance from their friends or guardians to perform their tasks. Our vision is to make a blind child feel like an able-bodied person who is capable enough to do almost all his daily tasks by himself without any help from anyone. The sightless students are unable to gain knowledge by reading books, surfing the internet, or taking online courses

Our Mission

We aim to provide a platform where they can learn from their surroundings, by meeting new people, reading books through OCR, listening to audiobooks, and web surfing.
