About God’s Eye

God’s eye is an AI-based web application for blind children to help them perform their everyday chores themselves using a voice assistant and without any other help from anyone. This platform helps them to interact with the environment, enables them to learn from their surrounding by identifying various things around them, and also aids them in recognizing their friends and family.
